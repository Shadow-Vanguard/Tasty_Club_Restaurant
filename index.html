Fresh. Hot. Consistently Good.

Located in the heart of Hor Al Anz, Near Abu Hail Metro Station, Dubai, Tasty Club Restaurant offers a diverse multi-cuisine dining experience built around convenience, affordability, and flavor. Known for its wide-ranging menu—from freshly grilled chicken and shawarma to Indian curries, Chinese dishes, burgers, and all-day breakfast options—there’s something for every craving at any hour.

With its 24/7 service, quick preparation, and consistently fresh meals, it’s a go-to spot for both quick bites and late-night dining. Guests appreciate the generous portions, budget-friendly pricing, and relaxed, casual atmosphere. Friendly and attentive staff further enhance the experience, making every visit smooth and satisfying.

Whether you're stopping by for a quick meal, ordering late at night, or looking for variety in one place, Tasty Club Restaurant delivers reliable, flavorful food around the clock.
